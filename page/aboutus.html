WARM Facility

The Water and Natural Resources Management (WARM) Facility supports the preparation and implementation of strategic infrastructure investment projects in the areas of water and natural resources management and urban resilience management in response to key local challenges such as flooding, inundation and coastal and riverbank erosion, saline invasion, exacerbated by climate change impacts.

WARM Projects

Project name:

Erosion prevention and sustainable coastal line protection for Hoi An, Quang Nam province

Project duration:

2023-2028

Lead agency:

Quang Nam Provincial People Committee

Implementing Agency:

Quang Nam Provincial Agriculture and Rural Development Project Management Unit (PMU)

Donors:

French Development Agency (AFD) and European Union (EU)

Total investment cost:

VND 1.149.511 million, which is equivalent to EUR 47 million (Exchange rate EUR 1 = VND 24,442).
